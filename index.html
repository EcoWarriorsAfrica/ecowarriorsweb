Empowering Africa Through Sustainability

A gamified platform empowering African youth to tackle climate change through missions, collaboration & impact tracking.

About the Project

🌍 Problem Statement

Youth across Africa lack engaging tools to learn and act on climate issues.

💡 Our Solution

EcoWarriors Africa is a gamified platform with missions, collaboration, and impact tracking.

📖 Project Overview

EcoWarriors Africa bridges the gap between awareness and action. By turning climate-positive activities—like tree planting, clean-ups, and advocacy—into engaging, point-earning missions, we motivate sustained participation and real-world impact.

Key Features

🎯

Gamified Missions

Complete real-world tasks to earn points, level up, and unlock badges.

🤝

Collaboration

Form teams, compete in challenges, and share progress with your community.

📊

Impact Tracking

Visualize collective achievements—trees planted, CO₂ offset, waste collected.

🏆

Rewards & Recognition

Redeem points for eco-friendly prizes or community grants.

How It Works

📝

1. Sign Up

Register with email or social login and set your goals.

🔍

2. Browse Missions

Filter by planting, clean-ups, awareness, and more.

✅

3. Complete & Verify

Submit photo proof or partner check-ins for verification.

🏅

4. Earn Points

Unlock badges and climb the leaderboards.

🎁

5. Redeem Rewards

Exchange points for eco-friendly prizes or fund local projects.
