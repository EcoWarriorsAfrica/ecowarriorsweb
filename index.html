About the Project

🌍 Problem Statement

Across Africa, many young people are passionate about the environment but lack interactive and engaging tools that empower them to learn about climate challenges and take meaningful action. Traditional education methods often fail to inspire sustained participation, leaving a gap between awareness and action in climate education..

💡 Our Solution

EcoWarriors Africa is a gamified platform where players complete eco-missions, collaborate with others, and track their environmental impact in real-time. By turning sustainable actions into interactive challenges— like delivering seedlings or collecting recyclables—the platform empowers users to learn, act, and make a difference while having fun.

📖 Project Overview

EcoWarriors Africa is an educational driving simulation game designed to bridge the gap between environmental awareness and real-world action. Players take on the role of EcoWarriors delivering essential green resources—such as seedlings, recycling bins, and solar kits—across African cities. By facilitating activities like tree planting, clean-ups, and renewable energy adoption, the game turns climate-positive actions into fun, mission-based gameplay. Through eco-tips, challenges, and environmental point systems, players not only learn sustainable habits but also see the tangible impact of their virtual efforts. EcoWarriors Africa inspires sustained engagement and empowers young Africans to become active stewards of the environment.

Key Features

🚚

Delivery Missions

Pilot your Eco‑vehicle through real‑world city streets—pick up seedling crates, recycling bins or solar kits, navigate traffic, avoid collisions and deliver on time to earn stars and Environmental Points.

💡

Educational Eco‑Tips

Learn as you drive: dynamic pop‑up facts (e.g. “Gentle braking can save up to 15% fuel”) appear in‑game to reinforce best practices in fuel efficiency, waste management and renewable energy.

📈

City Health Progression

Watch each city’s EcoHealth Meter rise as you complete missions—clear skies, greener parks and unlocked vehicles, new districts and story challenges await once you hit each milestone.

🏁

Competitive & Ghost Racing

Challenge your friends or your own best run: race head‑to‑head in online lobbies or against ghost vehicles, compare times and penalties on leaderboards, and chase daily or weekly route challenges.

🏆

Rewards & Upgrades

Spend your earned EP and green tokens in the EcoShop to unlock faster vehicles, improved cargo stability, special skins—and even fund real‑world tree‑planting partnerships.
