EcoWarriors Africa

An interactive driving game that empowers African youth to understand and tackle climate issues through fun, meaningful missions.

How it works

About the Project

We turn eco-awareness into habit-forming action using real city routes, playful challenges, and rewards that reflect impact.

🌍

Problem

Young people care about the environment, but textbooks and posters rarely build daily habits.

💡

Our Approach

Mission-based driving that rewards fuel-smart choices, waste sorting, and efficient routing.

🚗

Built for Africa

Local contexts, offline-friendly play, and practical EcoTips that match real streets.

EcoTips in-game

Your decisions power the EcoHealth meter — avoid idling, plan routes, and sort waste to unlock cleaner air, happier citizens, and new districts.

How It Works

Pick a city → accept missions → make eco-smart choices → watch EcoHealth rise. Compete with friends or your own best time.

Mission Types
